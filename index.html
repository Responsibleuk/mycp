Destinations

Malta and its surrounding islands offers yacht charters a fantastic combination of UNESCO world heritage ports; unspoilt anchorages; beautiful historical architecture from Neolithic temples and greek fables through to renowned Knights Templar as well as a vibrant night life in its cities.

Its long and consistent season, running from April to October, make it perfect for those wanting to enjoy everything synonymous with the best of mediterranean crusing. Its cyrstal blue waters, unspoilt sunkissed beaches, hidden coves and diving areas make it ideal for making full use of all the amenities on your charter yacht, whether a full super yacht, sports yacht or sailing yacht, you won’t be disappointed.

Itinieraries in Malta offer the opportunity to explore the main island, as well as the sister Isles of Gozo, famed home to Calypso in Homers Odyssey and beautiful car free unspoilt Camino, renowned for snorkling and diving with its own blue lagoon.

M/Y Charter Pro allows you to fully manage your booking throughout the pre-departure period, within the terms and conditions set by the yacht manager. In some regions it may be possible to charter a yacht without a license, however, regardless of whether you are an experienced sailor or not, chartering a yacht with a skipper means you not only have an expert sailor on hand, but also one who knows all the best spots, will plan everything for you, enabling you and your guests to simply relax an enjoy the experience.

The yacht listing will normally identify costs that are included or in addition to the charter rate.
